# Divine Blast Occultist - Path of Building Setup Guide

Since Divine Blast is a NEW skill in 3.28 Mirage League, there's no automated import code yet.
Follow these steps to create the build in Path of Building Community Fork:

## Step 1: Create New Build
1. Open Path of Building Community Fork (latest version)
2. Click "New"
3. Set Class: Witch
4. Set Ascendancy: Occultist
5. Set Level: 70 (for league start transition)

## Step 2: Import Passive Tree (Manual)
**Use this tree URL or build manually:**

**Level 70 Tree (Pre-Divine Blast):**
- Start at Witch
- Go towards Witch starting area
- Take: Arcane Potency, Deep Thoughts, Throatseeker
- Head to Templar area for Area nodes
- Take: Annihilation, Blast Radius, Arcane Expanse
- Grab life nodes: Purity of Flesh, Heart and Soul
- Occultist: Profane Bloom, Void Beacon

**Level 90 Tree (Full Build):**
- Above + Wicked Ward, Frigid Wake
- Add block nodes: Corrupted Block, Sanctuary
- More area damage nodes
- Eldritch Battery (if going CI)

## Step 3: Add Skills

### Main Skill (Replace with Divine Blast at 3.28 launch)
**Current placeholder:** Shield Throw
**Links:**
- Shield Throw
- Faster Attacks
- Added Fire Damage
- Elemental Damage with Attacks
- Inspiration
- Vicious Projectiles

### Divine Blast Setup (when available)
**Weapon:** Sceptre + Shield or Staff
**Links (6-link body armour):**
- Divine Blast
- Inspiration
- Concentrated Effect
- Controlled Destruction
- Energy Leech
- Increased Area of Effect (clear) / Concentrated Effect (bosses)

### Auras
- Herald of Thunder
- Herald of Ash
- Discipline (if ES based)
- Purity of Elements (early)
- Zealotry (if crit)

### Curses
- Warlord's Mark (on hit ring)
- or Assassin's Mark (if crit)

### Movement
- Shield Charge
- Flame Dash
- Faster Attacks (linked to Shield Charge)

### Golem
- Stone Golem (life regen)
- or Lightning Golem (cast speed)

## Step 4: Configure Items

### Weapon (Sceptre)
- Base: Platinum Sceptre or Karui Sceptre
- Stats: +1 to Strength Gems, Spell Damage, Cast Speed, Added Fire/Lightning

### Shield
- Base: Cardinal Round Shield (high armour)
- Stats: Life, Resists, Spell Block, +1 Strength Gems

### Body Armour
- Base: 5-link (early) → 6-link (late)
- Stats: Life, Resists, Energy Shield
- Color: B-B-B-R-G-G (for Divine Blast)

### Helmet
- Unique option: Crown of the Inward Eye
- Rare: Life, Resists, Energy Shield

### Gloves
- Stats: Life, Resists, Cast Speed
- Craft: "Curse enemies with Warlord's Mark on hit"

### Boots
- Stats: Life, Resists, Movement Speed (30%+)
- Enchant: Increased Cast Speed if you've been hit recently

### Belt
- Base: Leather Belt or Crystal Belt
- Stats: Life, Resists, Energy Shield

### Amulet
- Stats: Life, Cast Speed, Crit Multi, All Res
- Anoint: Blast Radius or Divine Judgement

### Rings
- Stats: Life, Resists, Cast Speed
- Craft: Curse on hit (Warlord's Mark)

## Step 5: Set Configuration

### Boss Settings
- Is the enemy a Boss? YES
- # of Primordial Golems summoned: 1
- Do you use Energy Shield? YES
- Are you always on Consecrated Ground? YES

### Skill Options
- Divine Blast: Release at maximum stages
- Inspiration: 5 stacks
- Area modifiers: YES

## Step 6: Check Stats

### Target Stats at Level 70
- Life: 3,500+
- Energy Shield: 1,000+ (or 5,000+ if CI)
- Resists: 75% all
- Cast Speed: 2.0+ casts/sec
- Area Damage: 200%+

### Target Stats at Level 90
- Life: 5,000+ / ES: 8,000+ (CI)
- Resists: 75% all, Chaos: 75% (CI) or positive
- Cast Speed: 3.0+ casts/sec
- Area Damage: 400%+
- Block: 75% attack, 50% spell
