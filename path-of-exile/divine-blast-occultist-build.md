# Divine Blast Occultist - 3.28 Mirage League Starter

## IMPORTANT: Divine Blast is a NEW skill in 3.28
Since PoB doesn't have Divine Blast yet, this build uses Shield Throw as placeholder.
Replace Shield Throw with Divine Blast when 3.28 launches.

## Path of Building Import
Paste this into PoB: https://pobb.in/divine-blast-occultist-placeholder

## Build Overview
- **Class:** Witch → Occultist
- **Main Skill:** Divine Blast (Strength/Intelligence)
- **Weapon:** Shield + Sceptre/Staff (when Holy skills support added)
- **Defense:** Energy Shield + Block + Occultist's Wicked Ward
- **Playstyle:** Channel holy beam, explosions scale with area

## Gem Links (6-Link Divine Blast)
Divine Blast - Inspiration - Concentrated Effect - Controlled Destruction
- Energy Leech - Increased Area of Effect (clear) / Concentrated Effect (bosses)

## Leveling (Acts 1-10)

### Act 1
- Pick up Freezing Pulse from quest
- Use Fire Trap for single target
- Rush to Merciless Lab for Profane Bloom

### Act 2
- Get Shield Charge for movement
- Add Herald of Thunder for clear
- Pick up Essence Drain as backup

### Act 3
- Get Shield Throw from Siosa (placeholder for Divine Blast)
- Link: Shield Throw - Faster Attacks - Added Fire Damage
- Rush to Cruel Lab for Void Beacon

### Act 4
- Switch to Armageddon Brand for leveling until Divine Blast available
- Get Stone Golem for regen
- Rush to Merciless Lab for Withering Presence

### Act 5-10
- Continue with Armageddon Brand or Storm Brand
- Stack life and resists
- Prepare gear for Divine Blast transition

## PoB Passive Tree (pobb.in link placeholder)
**Level 70 Transition Tree:**
- Witch start → Occultist
- Pick up all area nodes (Divine Blast is area-based)
- Get block nodes (Corrupted Block wheel)
- Life/ES hybrid until CI transition
- Wicked Ward for ES sustain

## Gear Priorities

### Weapon
- Sceptre with +1 to Strength Gems (until Divine Blast support confirmed)
- Spell damage, cast speed, added fire/lightning

### Shield
- High armour base
- Life, resists, spell block
- +1 to Strength Gems (if Divine Blast counts)

### Body Armour
- 5-link for early mapping
- Life, resists, energy shield
- Upgrade to 6-link Divine Blast setup

### Helmet/Gloves/Boots
- Life, resists, energy shield
- Look for area damage, cast speed
- Aim for 75% all resists by Act 10

### Jewelry
- Life, resists, attributes
- Cast speed, spell damage
- Energy shield if going CI later

### Flasks
1. Life Flask (Seething or Panicked)
2. Quicksilver Flask
3. Granite Flask (armour for block)
4. Basalt Flask (physical reduction)
5. Quicksilver or Diamond Flask

## Pantheon
- Major: Soul of Lunaris (projectile avoidance)
- Minor: Soul of Shakari (poison immunity)

## Ascendancy Order
1. Profane Bloom (chaos explosions on cursed enemies)
2. Void Beacon (nearby enemies have -20% chaos res)
3. Withering Presence (chaos damage over time immunity)
4. Frigid Wake (freeze/chill immunity, ES sustain)

## Transition to Divine Blast (Level 70+)
When you acquire Divine Blast gem:
1. Socket in 5-link chest
2. Use Inspiration + Controlled Destruction supports
3. Replace Shield Throw completely
4. Adjust tree for more area if needed
5. Stack cast speed and area damage

## Endgame Notes
- Divine Blast likely scales with: Area Damage, Spell Damage, Fire Damage
- Occultist's Profane Bloom + explosions = screen clear
- Can transition to CI (Chaos Innoculation) for endgame
- Consider block nodes if Divine Blast has block synergy
